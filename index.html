02 The mission

Bridging what's changing
and what's next.

OTV Education Conclave & Expo aims to bring together policymakers, educators and students under one roof — and bridge the gap between emerging educational trends and practical career guidance.

It is a platform for proactive engagement to shape the future, especially in the fast-evolving landscape of AI and technology-driven education. The event serves as a comprehensive platform for students, parents and educators to engage in meaningful discussions about career planning, life choices, and emerging fields.
